🎨 Option A: "Midnight Sapphire" — Design Rationale

  • Primary color shift: Emerald → Deep Sapphire Blue (#3B82F6). Blue conveys trust, stability, and financial sophistication — the standard in banking/fintech (think Xero, QuickBooks, Stripe).
  • Background: Navy-black (#060B18) instead of pure black (#09090b). Warmer, easier on the eyes, more premium feel. Surface levels use blue undertones (#0D1425, #131B30).
  • Semantic color separation: Gold (#F59E0B) for warnings/moderate, Green (#22C55E) for positive/growth, Coral (#F87171) for debt/negative, Teal (#14B8A6) as secondary accent. Each has a single job.
  • Chart hierarchy: Net Wealth is the bold solid line with gradient fill. Assets/Liabilities are subtle dashed lines — clearly secondary.
  • Metric cards: Left accent bars color-coded by meaning (blue=cashflow, green=savings, gold=debt-warning, green=surplus). Current design uses the same emerald accent everywhere.
  • Typography: Inter instead of Geist for sharper tabular numbers. 48px hero number with -2px tracking for impact.
  • Sidebar widget: Blue-tinted glass instead of emerald — matches the overall theme.
  • Spacing: More generous padding (32px content), 14px border-radius for modern feel, 4px metric bars (thinner = more refined).
  • Overall mood: Premium, institutional, "private banking dashboard" — builds trust with high-net-worth feel.
