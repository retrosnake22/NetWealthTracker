🎨 Option B: "Warm Minimal" — Design Rationale

  • Warm neutrals: #111111 base instead of cold blue-blacks or pure black. All grays use neutral tones (no blue or green tint). Feels like a high-end editorial layout — think Bloomberg Terminal meets Notion.
  • Primary: Teal (#0D9488 / #2DD4BF) — a fresh alternative to emerald. Slightly more sophisticated, less "health app". Still feels financial and trustworthy without being blue-generic.
  • Typography-first hero: No card container — the net wealth figure is displayed as a large typographic statement with a simple divider. Uses JetBrains Mono for numbers (true tabular spacing). 52px at -2px tracking = serious financial presence.
  • Connected stat strip: Instead of individual floating cards, the 4 KPIs sit in one unified row with dividers between them. This reduces visual noise and creates a clear "data bar" visual hierarchy. The eye scans left→right naturally.
  • Horizontal bar allocation: Replaces the donut/pie chart with a stacked horizontal bar + list. Easier to compare proportions, takes less vertical space, and works better at small sizes. Percentages are explicitly shown.
  • Minimal borders & shadows: 7% white borders only. No glow effects, no gradients on cards. The color comes from the data itself, not decorative elements. This is the "less is more" approach.
  • DM Sans + JetBrains Mono: DM Sans is geometric and clean for labels. JetBrains Mono is purpose-built for numbers — better than any proportional font for financial data. The pairing creates clear role distinction.
  • 3px thin metric bars: Current design uses 6px progress bars. Thinner = more refined, lets the number speak for itself. Each bar uses semantic color only (green, amber, red, teal).
  • Reduced color palette: Only 5 colors total — teal, green, amber/gold, red (soft), purple. Each has one clear semantic meaning. No competing shades. The warm grays do all the spatial work.
  • Overall mood: Calm, confident, editorial. Designed for daily use — nothing should be visually fatiguing. Content-forward, decoration-minimal.
